POWER OF ATTORNEY
Document No. WF-POA-2024-002 · WorkFlex Ltd, United Kingdom

This Power of Attorney ("PoA") is granted by WorkFlex Ltd, a company incorporated in England and Wales (Company No. GB 789 012 345), having its registered office at 22 Bishopsgate, London EC2N 4BQ, United Kingdom (hereinafter referred to as the "Principal").

The Principal hereby appoints WorkFlex GmbH, a company incorporated under German law, as its lawful attorney (hereinafter referred to as the "Attorney"), to act on its behalf in all matters relating to employment compliance, payroll administration, and regulatory filings within the jurisdiction of the United Kingdom.

The Attorney is authorised to:

  1. Execute and submit all necessary employment-related filings to HMRC and other relevant UK authorities;
  2. Manage and process payroll on behalf of the Principal for employees engaged under WorkFlex arrangements;
  3. Enter into agreements with third-party providers necessary for the execution of global mobility services;
  4. Represent the Principal in any administrative proceedings related to the above matters.

This Power of Attorney shall remain in force for a period of two (2) years from the date of execution, unless earlier revoked in writing by the Principal with thirty (30) days' notice.

This document is governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes arising hereunder shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.
